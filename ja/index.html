ようこそ RoyHarris.comへ

For decades, students around the globe have looked to me as a source of practical instruction rooted in efficiency, precision, and measurable results - as evidenced by having taught nearly 1,000 seminars in 24 countries. This updated version of my website continues that tradition with a collection of videos, articles and audio files on martial arts, self-defense, combatives, arrest & control, functional fitness and more. Take a look around, learn a few things, and even contact me to learn more!

お問い合わせ

私が提供するもの

対面レッスン

I teach private & semi-private lessons at my private training facility, in clients' homes as well as in their places of business.

オンラインレッスン＆コース

I teach private & semi-private lessons online through a variety of video formats (e.g. Messenger, Zoom, WhatsApp, etc.), as well as through my online courses.

国際/地域セミナー

Having taught nearly 1,000 seminars on Jeet Kune Do, Combatives, Arrest & Control, Jiu Jitsu, Kalis Ilustrisimo, and Functional Fitness, I am open to teaching seminars at different locations.

相談

相談 are done one-on-one as well as with larger groups. I address specific problems and together we come up with practical solutions that work for you.
