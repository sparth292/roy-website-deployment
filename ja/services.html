サービス
プライベート＆セミプライベート

Face-to-Face Consultations

Every successful training program begins with understanding the individual. During our consultation, we'll discuss your goals, frustrations, and the obstacles standing between you and the skills you want to develop. Together, we'll identify practical solutions, establish realistic expectations, and build a personalized plan designed to transform confusion into clarity, weaknesses into strengths, and practice into genuine competence and confidence.

サービス
San Diego & Southern California

In-Home Private Training

Rather than forcing you to adapt to a school's schedule, I bring individualized instruction directly to your home or business throughout Southern California (i.e., San Diego, Orange, LA, IE & Counties). Every lesson is customized around your goals, experience, learning style, and physical abilities. Whether your interest is self-defense, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Jeet Kune Do, Functional Fitness, or professional skills, my objective is simple: help you develop measurable competence and the confidence to apply it successfully.

サービス
Multiple Disciplines

Instructor Courses

Great instructors do far more than teach and demonstrate techniques—they develop capable practitioners who understand what they're teaching and why it works. My Instructor Courses provide structured pathways in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Harris Jiu Jitsu, Jeet Kune Do, Filipino Martial Arts, Arrest & Control, and more. Each certification level builds progressively upon the last, emphasizing technical proficiency, teaching methodology, leadership, and measurable standards of performance.

サービス
Worldwide via Video

Online Consultations

Distance has never been a barrier to quality instruction. Through online consultations with clients/students around the world, I will analyze your current abilities, identify obstacles, develop targeted training methods, and create practical lesson plans tailored to your goals. Regular follow-up sessions will allow us to measure progress, refine your training, solve new challenges, and steadily build both competence and confidence over time.

サービス
nearly 1000 Seminars in 24 Countries

Seminars & Workshops

Having taught nearly 1000 seminars and workshops in 24 countries, I've learned that every group has different goals, abilities, and challenges. Rather than delivering a one-size-fits-all presentation, I tailor each seminar to the needs of those attending. Participants leave with more than techniques—they gain practical skills, deeper understanding, improved decision-making, and greater confidence in their ability to perform under pressure.
